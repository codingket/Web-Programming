Welcome to NITW Eats

NITW Eats is a centralized platform designed to help students easily discover and explore food spots across the NIT Warangal campus. Instead of relying on word-of-mouth or wandering around, this website brings together all major stalls, cafes, and eateries in one place.

Whether you’re looking for a quick coffee between classes, a late-night snack near the hostels, or a proper meal at the food court, NITW Eats helps you find exactly what you need with minimal effort.

The goal is simple: reduce the time spent searching and increase the time spent enjoying. With a clean interface and structured layout, the platform is built to feel intuitive for every student.
